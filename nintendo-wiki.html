Wiki: A guide to jailbreaking and more

In this part of the Nintendo wiki, I will explain on how to jailbreak a Nintendo Switch, OLED version. (others not yet supported). Remember that you must have installed a physical modchip before starting to jailbreak this switch OLED. you can take it to an experienced person and/or read this wiki here. If you have installed a modchip on your switch OLED with PicoFly or other on, you may continue this wiki.







Preparing Files Needed for jailbreaking

Preparing Atmosphere, the CFW

Before we begin, we need to prepare our CFW (custom firmware), that allows us to do things that stock firmwares (original factory firmware) can't do. This wiki uses the Atmosphere CFW, because its the most commonly used among others. You can download atmospere CFW, using the link here. And make sure to use the latest CFW.
